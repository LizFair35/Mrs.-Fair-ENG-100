Course Requirements:

  • Remember that the ECHS/MSU code of conduct and policies apply to this course.
  • First and foremost, every student must have a Chromebook/Laptop in order to successfully complete the course. Please check our Google classroom daily for announcements and assignments.
  • MLA documentation is required for all papers.
    Plagiarism is uncalled for and will not be tolerated.
  • If plagiarism is suspected and purposeful, you will not get credit for the assignment and your name will be submitted to a committee at MSU who will decide the action to take against you.
    • (Read the Academic Integrity section of the syllabus).
  • All major assignments must be completed to pass the class. All final drafts will be submitted through our Google Classroom no later than midnight on the due date. Each essay will have certain requirements which I will give you well before the due date.
  • Drafts/Peer Reviews and Discussion Board responses will be credited as 25/0. In other words, if you do them, you receive full credit (25) and if not, you will receive a 0. Disclaimer: if you do not submit your discussion board post by 11:59 pm of the due date, you will receive a 0.
    • Peer reviews require that you be in class the day of the review unless you make prior arrangements.
  • Discussion Board responses should be at least 500 words and you must respond to at least two of your peersâ€™ responses; you must do both of these in order to receive full credit.
  • In the event of unforeseeable technology circumstances, PLEASE work on your assignments early and save often. ALWAYS have a backup copy of your work saved on a USB drive and/or on Google Drive. If you have ANY technology questions or concerns, PLEASE discuss them with me well before the due date and I will work with you. If you are worried an assignment did not submit, ALWAYS e-mail me a copy before the due date so I will have it.
  • E-mail policy: Students will need to send any papers, questions, issues, to my ECHS email during appropriate hours. Please expect a response from me during the 8:00 am to 3:00 pm time frame.
